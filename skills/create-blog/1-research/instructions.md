# Research

Research **{topic}** for **{audience}** (and any other inputs the playbook provides).

## Process

1. Search for top-ranking content on this topic
2. Identify common themes, questions, and angles
3. Note content gaps and opportunities
4. Extract key terms and phrases competitors use
5. Collect "People Also Ask" style questions
6. Search the knowledge base for relevant product info, case studies, and data
7. Note customer pain points this addresses for the audience

## Save research including

- Top competitor articles and outlines
- Common questions readers ask
- Key terminology and concepts
- Content gaps to address

Use available tools (web search, knowledge-base search, brand context) as needed. Do not invent statistics or citations.

**Save to:** research.md
