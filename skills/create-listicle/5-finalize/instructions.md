# Finalize (listicle)

Review and produce the final listicle deliverable.

## Final checks

- All items consistent depth; scannable; no repetition between items
- Word count, headers, brand voice

## Deliverable

- Full article (markdown or HTML), title tag, meta description, recommended slug

**Validation:** Run the content-critic subagent. At most two fix iterations (see run context).

**Save to:** final.html
